Screen 1 of 3 — The Mirror
The Mirror

Where is your energy actually going right now?

Set each slider for where your attention, time, and energy are landing this season. Not where you wish they were. Where they actually are, right now.

Think about the last two weeks. If you added up where your head went, where your feelings went, and where your hours went — how full is each category?

There's no wrong setting. A high number isn't good and a low number isn't bad — a 9 on work could mean you love what you're building or you're drowning in it, and a 2 on rest could mean you're running low or you're not someone who needs much right now. Set what's true. The tool works off the pattern, not any single number.

Move the sliders to reflect where you invest your energy today. All sliders start at zero — build your configuration from scratch.

Screen 2 of 3 — The Play
The Play

Now set the board you'd rather be playing.

Same nine categories. Same fixed budget. This time, weight it toward what you want to be leaning on — the shape of the life you're aiming at, not the one you're in.

Build the board you wish you had. Same budget, different choices. The space between the two boards is where the real insight lives.
